Welcome to the SpaceMonkeys' project homepage! Every year students in the "Introduction to Software Engineering" module of Warwick University's Computer Science course must participate in a group project. We were one of those groups!

Our year's task (2002/2003) was to produce a train timetabeling system. The full assignment is here (Note: This link is only accessible from within the uni network!). The members of the best team will receive 50 quid and a get to go to IBM to compete against groups from other universities for a chance to win an IBM Laptop each! We really want/need the money/laptops so we were gonna try damn hard to be the best team! Raaarrr!

The SpaceMonkeys rock forever!

Part of our work was to produce design specifications, minutes of group meetings and other documentation regarding the project. We decided to make all our documents in HTML so that they would be easily maintainable and accessible (see our first meeting's minutes). Since these are readable by any browser we put our work online on this site so that we would have a central information resource, which helped us streamline our work!.

The project has now been completed (but the winners have not been decided yet). You can download our finished program and its manual. We are keeping this site online partly for nostalgia value and incase it is of use and/or interest to software engineering groups in future years. Since there is no longer nay need for secrecy our internal documents are now publicly accessible.

Christmas Celebrations

O-Block is so cool, that it even has Christmas twice a year! Since we break up before for holidays before the 'real' Christmas in December we decided to have a second Christmas in November so that we can celebrate together!
We've all had Advents Calenders since the 1st of November (Cooking Alex has already eaten all of his, actually!), we've started putting up decorations in the kitchen and in our rooms and on the 25th we'll have a proper Christmas dinner. Mmmmmhhh!
